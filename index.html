In the heart of Pumpkinville, there lived a special 10-year-old boy named Jack. He had a pumpkin for a head that glowed with warmth and a youthful spirit. Every evening, his pumpkin head came to life, and he embarked on an adventure! On his journey, he'd traverse haunted streets, spooky cities, and eerie forests filled with friendly and not-so-friendly entities.


Edwin is the epitome of adorable, with a round, translucent, and almost cloud-like appearance. He's always seen sporting a friendly grin that'll melt your heart, and his bright, twinkling eyes exude warmth and kindness. Edwin was once a mischievous ghost who just couldn't seem to spook anyone. He'd try to rattle chains, make spooky sounds, and sneak up on people, but they'd just chuckle and call him adorable. One fateful Halloween, he got tangled in his own bedsheet costume, tripped, and bumped into a candy bowl, showering trick-or-treaters with sweets. The laughter that followed was so infectious that it made him realize his true calling was to spread joy, not fear. Now, he's determined to make people smile instead of shriek.


Harold is the polar opposite of friendly. With his mean eyes, gaping maw, and an eerie, whispery voice, he exudes an aura of malevolence. Harold was once a greedy, unpleasant individual in the land of the living. He spent his days scheming and swindling others out of their hard-earned money, and his nights devising mean-spirited pranks to torment his neighbours. When he met his untimely demise during a particularly wicked prank, he became a vengeful spirit, and now accosts travellers that might happen upon his ghostly gathering grounds.
